# Newsroom Poster

## Use When

Use for launches, public updates, incidents, dates, and fast-moving news summaries.

## Visual DNA

Deadline energy, strong headline field, stamp-like marks, paper/news texture, high contrast without pure black.

## Must Use

- Clear headline hierarchy.
- Date or update energy when relevant.
- Strong image or number anchor.

## Must Not Use

- Fake article paragraphs.
- Tabloid sensationalism.
- Too many stamp effects.

## Prompt Skeleton

"Create a finished newsroom poster information card with a strong headline field, one date or key fact, deadline energy, tactile paper texture, restrained stamp marks, and one clear visual anchor. Avoid fake newspaper paragraphs, tabloid styling, and excess stamps."

## Layout Pairings

Best with `full-bleed-poster`, `big-number`, `contact-sheet`, and `artifact-board`.

## Text Strategy

Bold headline plus one short subline, date, or key fact. Avoid fake newspaper body text.

## QA Checks

- Feels urgent but credible.
- No fake article columns.
- Headline and key fact are large, clean, and readable.

## Repair Prompts

- "Remove fake newspaper body text and keep one bold headline."
- "Reduce stamp marks to one or two credible update marks."
- "Make the poster feel authoritative, not tabloid."
