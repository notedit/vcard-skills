# Image-Led Magazine

## Use When

Use for premium editorial card sets where images carry narrative, mood, and pacing.

## Visual DNA

Strong photography or synthetic stills, purposeful crop, visual rhythm, restrained editorial color, large negative space.

## Must Use

- A primary image moment.
- Crop or sequencing that feels selected.
- Minimal but confident text.

## Must Not Use

- Generic stock-like people or scenes.
- Decorative magazine borders and fake issue labels.
- Low-contrast atmospheric filler.

## Prompt Skeleton

"Create a finished image-led magazine information card with a strong headline, concise evidence labels, a selected image moment, premium editorial crop, and restrained color. The image supports the message. Avoid stock-photo blandness, fake magazine chrome, and decorative filler."

## Layout Pairings

Best with `contact-sheet`, `product-specimen`, and `full-bleed-poster`.

## Text Strategy

Headline plus labels. Use a strong title and up to 3 concise captions or evidence labels. Keep labels outside image frames where possible.

## QA Checks

- Image is specific and inspectable.
- Crop creates momentum.
- Required title/labels are readable and no fake magazine issue text appears.

## Repair Prompts

- "Make the crop bolder and remove decorative magazine chrome."
- "Replace stock-like atmosphere with a specific visual moment: <MOMENT>."
- "Increase image dominance and reduce captions."
